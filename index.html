The World of Kelrûn

In the year 0 AR, the First Necromancer shattered the Veil—the sacred barrier between life and death. What was once a natural cycle became a corrupting force that twisted the world of Kelrûn forever.

Now, seven powerful factions battle for dominance in a land where death has been broken. From the skeletal legions of the Bone March to the anti-necromancy crusaders of the DawnFlame Order, each faction represents a different path in this world of eternal struggle.

Choose your side wisely, for in Kelrûn, power comes at a terrible price.
