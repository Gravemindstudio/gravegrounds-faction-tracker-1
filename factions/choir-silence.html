Faction Lore

When the First Necromancer's scream tore the Veil, not all the dead clawed upward. Some sank inward, becoming hollow souls who could not find their names, ghosts severed from memory, purpose, or prayer.

They drifted across the shattered fields of Viremyr, minds leaking from broken bodies, voices slipping through torn mouths. In the ruins of the Echoing Hollow, these broken spirits found each other. Their pain did not heal - it harmonized. Thus, the Choir of Silence was born.

Abilities & Powers

  • Memory Erasure: Unmake names, memories, and identities
  • Silent Resonance: Hum commands that cause weapons to rust and minds to forget
  • Psychic Erosion: Dissolve defenses through the loss of belief and meaning
  • Ritual Unmaking: Perform ceremonies that erase entire cities from memory
  • Silent Lords: Necromancers who have surrendered identity to wield silence

Leadership

Maelrith the Stillborn

The oldest of the Silent Lords, his veiled form a living wound in reality. He has abandoned not just his name, but all memory of his origin.

Choirmaster of Silence

Territory

The Choir claims no territory as its own, but where they linger, the world becomes their domain. They operate from Sanctuaries of Silence - ruined cathedrals, forsaken temples, and echoing halls where sound is deadened and memory is unwound. These places exist in the borderland between life and death.

Faction Philosophy

"Language is a lie. Memory is a shackle. Identity is a weakness. We see the First Necromancer's scream not as rage, but as the first hymn - a call to forget, to erase grief by erasing the self. We do not destroy the world through fire or blood. We destroy it by unmaking meaning itself."
